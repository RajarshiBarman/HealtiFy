Dengue

Dengue, a mosquito-borne viral infection, poses a significant public health concern globally. Characterized by flu-like symptoms, Dengue is transmitted through the bites of infected Aedes mosquitoes, primarily Aedes aegypti. This infectious disease is prevalent in tropical and subtropical regions, making it a persistent threat to many communities.
